# measures

The raw river level measures.  The continuous graphs illustrate each gauge station's raw measures vis-à-vis device measurement-recording-frequency; usually every _15_ minutes.  The split graphs allow for annual comparisons of measurements.  Due to measuring device faults, etc., a gauge's raw measurements may include erroneous measures, or measures might be missing; [study the anomalies section](../detection/index.md){ data-preview }.
