# expert

This zone outlines expert concepts and the attributes/profiles of the hub's models.  In relation to models, a key aim of ANONYMOUS is transparent models; black boxes are evitable choices.  Subject experts can use the attributes/profiles of a model to determine the veracity of a model's estimates, forecasts, etc.
